Welcome to VoiLoad

Voice Message Downloader for Facebook / Messenger

⚠️

Important Notice

Before first use, please refresh your Facebook / Messenger page.

Or click "Get started" button below.

How to Use

1

Go to Facebook or Messenger

Open the conversation with voice messages you want to download

2

Right-click on Voice Message

Check the contextmenu, find "Download Voice Message"

3

Select "Download Voice Message"

Click "Save" in popup to download voice message

After clicking the button, you'll be directed to Facebook / Messenger.
